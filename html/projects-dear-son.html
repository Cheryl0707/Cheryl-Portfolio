DEAR SON

A short film showcasing on FilmFreeway Festival. The story about a man who used to be excellent has suffered from the passing of the family and indulged himself. While suddenly, an unexpected letter from his mom start to make the difference gradually.

- Project Type: Short Film Production

- Roles: Script Writer / Storyboarding / Sound Designer / Caption Translator

- Date: Nov - Dec, 2021

- Tools: Scenarist / Procreate / Adobe Premiere Pro

In the New Media Images course (IAT202), we worked as a team of 4 for a month to created a short film with a deeply personal purpose. As a group of international students far from our families, we yearned for the opportunity to stay connected with our loved ones. These emotions inspired our short film, 'DEAR SON’.

We build a touching story of a man who used to be excellent and has suffered from the passing of the family and indulged himself. While suddenly, an unexpected letter from his mom starts to make a difference gradually. The letter is a heartfelt expression of how much she misses her son and her profound regret that they can no longer be together. In the latter half of the film, these words serve as an apology not only from the character's mother but also from our production team, as we also experiencing the pain of being far from our own parents.
