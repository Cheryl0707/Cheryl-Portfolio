What is the story about?

The story is about a robot who have the ability to design collaborate with a female designer who is about to be fired to finish the last champian. During their collaboration, the secrets of the robot are unraveled.

Characters

We have human characters and robot characters. The robots are designed and modelled by our team members: Zhengji Li and Steven Han. And the human characters are construct from metahuman. The facial expressions are face captured in Unreal Engine.
