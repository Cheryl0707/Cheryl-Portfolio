Haunted Love

A project integrate first-person interactive virtual reality with narration to give player a transformative experience of domestic violence. The in-scene flashbacks will bring players to the back memories.

- Project Type: Interactive Virtual Reality Experience

- Team member: Ruijie Liu, Yuting Hu, Zhengji Li, Minyang Zhang, Ray Ho, Justin Yu, Vivian Hsu

- Roles: Researcher / Script Writer / Interaction Designer / Video editing (Trailer & Final descriptive video)

- Date: Mar - Apr 2023

- Tools: Unity VR/ Adobe Premiere Pro / Figma

Motivation

According to a report by UN Women (2021) on violence against women during COVID-19, 45% of women reported that they or a women they know has experienced a form of violence against women since the pandemic. 7 in 10 women felt that domestic violence has increased since the COVID-19 pandemic (p. 5).

We want to develop a Virtual Reality narration to create a transformative experience for the player to bring awareness to the topic of domestic violence. The journey starts with the traumatized victim revisits their old childhood. The progression of the storyline would take the players on a roller-coaster ride and experience the dark side of domestic violence, as well as walking out of the shadows along with the protagonist as they recovers from the traumatic past.

What helps the Immersion in Haunted Love?

Narration

The player visit their childhood house as it goes up for sale. In order to determine which areas of the house need to be renovated, he wander around the house. As he reminisces about his childhood, he begins to relive memories from that time.

Interaction Planning

The player can directly interact with most of the objects in the scene, they can trigger in-scene dialogues. Three of them are symbolized objects that can trigger memory flashbacks. These memories represent a violence, they become increasingly serious as the narrative progresses. The complete story will be gradually revealed as the audience explores and progresses.
