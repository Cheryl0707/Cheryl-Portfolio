Musical Recital

The musical recital sought to integrate nature into our design ethos, creating an open space which forging an interior space that remains bathed in the sun's gentle glow throughout the day.

- Project Type: Spatial Design

- Team member: Ruijie Liu, Kejing Ren, Yuting Hu

- Roles: Pergola Designer / 3D Modeller / Twinmotion Operator / Video Editor

- Date: July - August 2022

- Tools: Twinmotion (Epic games virtual Engine) / Rhino / Grasshopper / Procreate

Design principle

Main Circulation

The circulation is designed to be random, which allowing visitors to explore freely without prescribed pathways or predetermined routes. We roughly divide the space as inside and outside of the pavilion which only has a main level with centric circulation for visitors. They are all allowed to enter the space from multiple entrances. We have one main gate at the front, and two optional entrances guided by the pergola. The piano is at the center of the pavilion so that visitors can enjoy the music from any angle and position.

Final Presentation

We have used different materials in our design, including glass, and different types of woods. We want to create a comfortable and environmental-friendly place, that is consistent with the surroundings. We use marble as the main texture of pavilion. It‚Äôs a highly durable material and waterproof. Pergolas are made of wood which is also a durable and relatively low-cost material. Other than that, we have used glass as the wall materials on the pavilion, instead of sealing it up with wood or other materials.

We want to give users an open experience, and we think that glass as a see-through material allows users to interact with natural light and the environment. However, we have also considered the privacy and closed space as a pavilion, and so we have added light pink color onto the glass, to give users a bit closed and safe feeling. it adds more warm-tone to the recital hall as a whole.
